Projects:

Getting a Grip on Graduation with Machine Learning

Spring 2020
I trained Random Forest and Neural Net models on publically available Virginia high school graduation data in order to identify factors influencing students' decision to leave school early. With a tuned neural network model, we identified three important factors that the model relied on heavily when predicting dropout rates.

Python, Scikit-learn, Tensorflow

Ionization and its Influence on Forming Solar Systems

2018 - 2020
I produced a grid of numerical models with Fortran and C, and created a Python tool for manipulating and visualizing the output. Using the tool I made, I undertook a detailed data modeling effort to measure the influence ionization has on the evolution of a young solar system, IM Lup.

C, Fortran, Python, Pandas

Slaying Hydra: A Python Analysis Pipeline for the Hydra Spectrograph

2017 - 2018
I created a data processing pipeline in Python which is used to extract spectroscopic data from the Hydra spectrograph. The pipeline substantially expedites data extraction and analysis, of hundreds of stars, which are being studied as part of a larger project to understand how solar systems form.

Python

Interstellar Gas as a Diagnostic of Galaxy Clustering in the Early Universe

2016 - 2018
I combined data from a number of different sources and performed a statistical analysis to estimate gas depletion in two ancient, massive galaxy cluster progenitors.

Python, IDL

Finding Simple Observables to Predict White Dwarf Internal Structure

2014 - 2015
I automated the production of a suite of numerical models simulating the time series brightness of pulsating white dwarf stars. Using the simulations I produced, I validated a method for characterizing white dwarf stars using limited observational data.

Python, Matplotlib
